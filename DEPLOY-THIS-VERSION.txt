DEPLOY THE WEBSITE
1. Delete or replace the old files in your GitHub/Vercel project.
2. Upload ALL files and folders from this ZIP, including assets and vercel.json.
3. Confirm consultation.html references:
   - assets/google-sheet-config-final-v5.js
   - assets/pages-final-v4.js
4. Confirm index.html references:
   - assets/google-sheet-config-final-v5.js
   - script-final-v4.js
5. Commit and wait for the Vercel deployment to finish.
6. Open the newest deployment and press Ctrl + Shift + R once.
