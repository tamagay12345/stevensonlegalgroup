Service Standards

The qualities the firm aims to provide in every accepted matter.

01

Respectful communication

Clients should be able to ask questions and receive explanations that are understandable, direct and relevant to their circumstances.

02

Careful preparation

Legal positions should be based on the available facts, documents, evidence and applicable legal principles.

03

Realistic expectations

No responsible lawyer can guarantee an outcome. Clients should receive an honest assessment of risks, options and possible next steps.
