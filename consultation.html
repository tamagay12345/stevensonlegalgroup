Consultation Questions

Helpful information before the first conversation.

What should I prepare before a consultation?

Prepare a short timeline of important events, identify any urgent dates and gather the names of the people or organizations involved. Do not send confidential documents until the firm confirms that it can receive them.

Does submitting the form mean the firm represents me?

No. A lawyer-client relationship is created only after the firm completes the necessary review, agrees to accept the matter and confirms the relationship through an appropriate written retainer.

Can the firm consider matters outside Ontario?

Cross-border and international inquiries may be considered. The ability to provide legal services depends on the jurisdiction, the nature of the matter and applicable licensing requirements. Locally authorized counsel may be required.

What happens after the request is submitted?

The request is delivered through the visitor’s email application. The firm can then review the general nature of the inquiry and determine whether further information or a consultation is appropriate.

Prefer to contact the firm directly by email?

The contact page provides the firm’s primary and alternate email addresses.
