About the Firm

Representation grounded in preparation, integrity and trust.

Stevenson Legal Group LLP is an Ontario-based law firm committed to helping individuals, families and businesses navigate important legal matters with confidence.

Legal problems often arrive during uncertain or deeply personal moments. Our role is to bring structure to that uncertainty. We carefully assess the facts, explain the law in understandable terms and develop a focused plan that reflects each client’s priorities.

The firm combines strong advocacy with professional judgment. Where negotiation offers the most sensible path, we work toward a practical resolution. When firm litigation is required, we prepare deliberately and advocate with resolve.

Stevenson Legal Group is identified as a certified Minority Business Enterprise. Certification details should be made available to prospective clients upon request.

Barrister Jeanette Stevenson

Barrister · Stevenson Legal Group LLP

Born and raised in Canada, Jeanette Stevenson brings a calm, disciplined and highly personal approach to legal representation.

Jeanette understands that clients are rarely looking for legal theory alone. They need clear answers, practical options and an advocate who recognizes what is at stake. Her approach begins with listening closely, identifying the central legal and personal concerns and creating a strategy suited to the client’s goals.

Professional honesty is central to her work. Clients receive a straightforward assessment of the strengths, risks and possible paths in their matter. Jeanette does not rely on unnecessary complexity or unrealistic promises. She focuses instead on preparation, sound judgment and the strongest lawful position available under the circumstances.

Her head office is located in Canada. International and cross-border inquiries may be considered where appropriate, with locally licensed counsel engaged when the applicable jurisdiction requires it.

Family & Marriage Law

Practical, compassionate advice for separation, parenting, support and other important family transitions.

Protecting your future while addressing what matters now

Family disputes can affect your children, finances, home and sense of stability. Stevenson Legal Group approaches these matters with care, discretion and a clear understanding that no two families are alike.

The firm helps clients understand their legal rights and responsibilities, organize the information needed for informed decisions and evaluate negotiation, mediation and litigation options.

  • Separation and divorce planning
  • Parenting time and decision-making issues
  • Child and spousal support concerns
  • Property division and financial disclosure
  • Marriage and separation agreements
  • Urgent motions and protective legal steps

Practice 02

Criminal Defence

Prompt, discreet defence advice when your liberty, reputation and future may be at risk.

Early preparation can shape the direction of a defence

Being investigated, arrested or charged can be overwhelming. It is important to understand the allegations, the court process and the decisions that may affect your case before taking action.

Stevenson Legal Group examines the available evidence, procedural history and legal issues, while helping clients prepare for each stage of the process.

  • Pre-charge and investigation advice
  • Bail and release-related matters
  • Evidence and disclosure review
  • Charter and procedural issues
  • Negotiation and resolution discussions
  • Trial preparation and courtroom advocacy
How the firm helps

We respond promptly, preserve confidentiality, test the prosecution’s position and prepare a defence based on the evidence and applicable law.

Real Estate & Property Law

Clear guidance for property transactions, ownership concerns and real estate disputes.

Protecting the legal and financial value of your property decisions

Real estate matters involve important deadlines, detailed documents and significant financial commitments. A careful legal review can identify issues before they develop into costly disputes.

  • Agreement and document review

  • Boundary, access and easement issues
  • Failed transactions and contract disputes

Practice 04

Civil Litigation

Strategic representation for contract, financial, business and property-related disputes.

A disciplined strategy for resolving serious disputes

Litigation requires more than filing documents. It requires a clear theory of the case, reliable evidence, realistic risk assessment and a plan for each stage of the proceeding.

Stevenson Legal Group evaluates the legal and commercial considerations affecting the dispute, including cost, timing, evidence, enforceability and settlement opportunities.

  • Contract and payment disputes
  • Business and commercial claims
  • Property-related litigation
  • Negligence and financial loss claims
  • Demand letters and settlement negotiation
  • Motions, hearings and trial preparation
How the firm helps

We identify the strongest available arguments, build the evidence carefully and pursue a proportionate resolution through negotiation or litigation.
