Attorney Profile

Barrister Jeanette Stevenson

Barrister · Stevenson Legal Group LLP

Born and raised in Canada, Jeanette Stevenson brings a calm, deliberate and highly personal approach to legal representation.

Jeanette understands that clients do not seek legal assistance merely to receive technical information. They need an advocate who will listen carefully, identify what is truly at stake and help them make informed decisions during a difficult or uncertain period.

Her work begins with a detailed assessment of the circumstances. She considers the available documents, evidence, legal issues, deadlines and practical consequences before recommending a strategy. Clients are given a clear explanation of the available options and the risks associated with each path.

Jeanette places particular importance on professional honesty. She does not rely on unrealistic guarantees or unnecessary legal complexity. Her objective is to provide clients with a candid assessment and pursue the strongest responsible position available under the facts and applicable law.

Confidentiality and discretion are central to her practice. Family disputes, criminal allegations, financial concerns, property matters and estate issues often involve highly sensitive information. Clients can expect those concerns to be treated with care and respect.

Her head office is located in Ontario, Canada. International and cross-border inquiries may be considered where appropriate. Work outside Ontario is subject to applicable licensing requirements and may involve cooperation with locally authorized counsel.
